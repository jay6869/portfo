Channel State Information has quietly become the sensing fabric behind a generation of
presence and gesture products. This post documents what happens when you treat it as
untrusted input.

## Setup

One ESP32-S3 as a passive sniffer, a second as a crafted-frame transmitter, both clocked
against the same AP beacon. A laptop captures CSI snapshots for offline comparison.

The discipline that made this work was beacon timing — without a shared reference, injected
frames land outside the window the sensor samples and the whole thing is just noise.
